1) first launch the world with -> roslaunch amr-ros-config project_world.launch

2) use reset robot -> rosrun amr-ros-config reset_robot.py

3) open traffic sign detector -> rosrun amr-ros-config traffic_detector_2.py

4) launch Gmapping SLAM -> roslaunch amr-ros-config slam_gmapping.launch

Through RViz, Add->Map and the use topic /map to visualize mapping

5) Start moving the robot by, rosrun amr-ros-config navigator_3.py
